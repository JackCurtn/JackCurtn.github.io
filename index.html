About

Hi, my name is Jack and this is my portfolio.

I am currently working as a data analyst with Eli Lilly in Cork. I recently completed a Bachelor of Science in Software Development from Atlantic Technological University. This degree taught me about the fundamentals of software development, including how to create a software solution from the very beginning until the final product is delivered, including how to test software and best design practices.

Prior to finishing this degree, I obtained a Master's in Business Analytics, which allowed me to develop the skills needed to excel in the fields of data analytics/science/engineering and business analytics. Due to the project heavy nature of this course, and its focus on developing both quantitative analysis and consultancy skills, I feel this goal has been achieved.

My main interests include mountain biking, archery and Formula 1. I have a Lapierre hardtail mountain bike, which I take out as often as I can around the Ballyhoura trails in Co. Cork. Archery has been an interest of mine for the past few years, and I also have my own recurve bow and was elected as the public relations officer for my university's archery club. Formula 1 has become my most recent interest, and my favourite driver is Charles Leclerc!
