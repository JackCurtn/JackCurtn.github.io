About

Hi, my name is Jack and this is my portfolio.

I am currently completing a master's in Business Analytics from the University of Limerick, as such I will be available to work from the 8th of August. I chose to do this master's course as I wanted to develop the skills need to excel in the fields of data analytics/science/engineering. Due to the project heavy nature of this course, and it's focus on developing both quantitative analysis and consultancy skills, I feel my goal has been achieved, and as such I am seeking openings in the data industry.

My main interests include mountain biking, archery and Formula 1. I own a Lapierre hardtail mountain bike, which I take out as often as I can around the Ballyhoura trails in Co. Cork. Archery has been an interest of mine for the past few years, and I also have my own recurve bow and I am the public relations officer for my university's archery club. Formula 1 has become my most recent interest, and my favourite driver is Charles LeClerc!
